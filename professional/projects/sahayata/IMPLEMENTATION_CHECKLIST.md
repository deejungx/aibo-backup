## 🚀 Launch Week (Monday - Friday, Sprint 1)

### Monday 9 AM: Sprint Planning
- [ ] Meeting scheduled, calendar sent
- [ ] Pre-filled sprint doc ready (retro insights + backlog)
- [ ] Arun has top 3 signals written down
- [ ] Run planning meeting (30 min max)
  - Recap retro insights (2 min)
  - Arun presents signals (5 min)
  - Team confirms sprint goal (3 min)
  - Finalize assignments (10 min)
  - Check blockers (5 min)
- [ ] Post sprint goal + backlog publicly (Slack, board, wherever)

### Tue-Fri: Daily Posts
- [ ] Remind team about async daily posts (Slack thread)
- [ ] Post your own daily post as example (set culture)
- [ ] Spot-check: Are people posting? If not by Wed, nudge once

### Thursday 9 AM: Mid-week Huddle
- [ ] Pull blockers from daily posts before meeting
- [ ] Someone demos what shipped (you or team lead)
- [ ] Unblock any issues raised
- [ ] Keep it to 30 min

### Friday 9 AM: Sprint Retro
- [ ] Run retro meeting (30 min)
  - What went well (5 min)
  - What was hard (5 min)
  - Kudos round (10 min) — this is important, don't skip
  - One improvement for next sprint (5 min)
  - Pack up kudos in permanent doc
- [ ] Document: What worked? What felt clunky?

### Friday (After Retro)
- [ ] Team retrospective on the *rituals themselves*
  - Was 9 AM time OK?
  - Did daily posts happen?
  - Did kudos feel real?
  - Did team feel connected?
- [ ] Note: One thing to tweak for Sprint 2

## ⚠️ Failure Mode Watch List

**If this is happening, course-correct:**

| Problem | Symptom | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Meetings feel like overhead | People missing syncs, checked out during retro | Cut ruthlessly. Use Thursday huddle instead of sync if less critical. Make retro 20 min instead of 30. |
| Kudos feel fake | Silence during kudos round, generic praise | Make it optional Sprint 1. Model specific kudos yourself. Keep it real, not forced. |
| Daily posts die off | Nobody posting by mid-sprint | Make it optional. Accept that some people prefer reading. Don't make it a chore. |
| Sprint goals feel disconnected from customers | Team says "I don't know why we're building this" | Arun needs to bring stronger signals. Add customer quote to sprint goal. Make the connection explicit. |
| Two orgs still feel siloed | Symbiosis doing one thing, Telerivet another | Rotate who leads rituals. Call out cross-org kudos. Assign people from different orgs to same sprint goal. |
| Backlog runs empty mid-sprint | Team finishes early, nothing to work on | Arun + 1 person refine 3-4 items mid-sprint (async doc, no meeting). Build buffer backlog. |

## 🎯 Remember

- **You're building culture, not process.** The rituals are the container. The real work is creating a team that ships faster and feels good about it.
- **Lean now, elaborate later.** Start with 90 min/week. If team asks for more, add it. If not, keep it lean.
- **Customer-driven solves 80% of arguments.** When prioritization is unclear, ask Arun. Don't debate gut feel.
- **Kudos matter more than you think.** People remember who appreciated them. It matters.
- **Distributed teams need written things.** Async docs for signals, retro notes, kudos. They're your memory.

You've got this. Start Monday.
