# Sahayata - Critical Governance Issues

**Date:** February 16, 2026

## 🚨 IP Ownership - BLOCKING ISSUE

**Status:** Unresolved between Symbiosis Solutions and Telerivet

**The Problem:**
- Sahayata was built organically by both teams collaborating
- Telerivet provides the omnichannel platform infrastructure
- Symbiosis provides product vision + engineering (Looza)
- No formal agreement exists on who owns the product, IP, or future revenue

**Why This Matters:**
- Can't raise funding without clarity (investors need to know who owns what)
- Can't close enterprise customers with confidence (who signs the contract?)
- Can't scale/hire without knowing equity structure
- If partnership dissolves, Sahayata becomes contested territory

**What Needs to Happen:**
1. Define ownership structure:
   - Is Sahayata a **Symbiosis product** that uses Telerivet's platform?
   - Or is it a **joint venture** with both parties?
   - Or does **Telerivet own it** and Symbiosis is a contractor?
   
2. Define revenue/margin split:
   - Who keeps customer revenue?
   - What percentage goes to platform costs (Telerivet)?
   - What's left for product development?
   
3. Get legal clarity:
   - IP assignment agreement
   - Revenue sharing terms
   - Exit/dissolution clause
   - Partner role definitions

**Recommended Path:**
- Telerivet likely wants equity stake or revenue share (not just their platform usage fee)
- Symbiosis likely wants IP control (own company asset)
- **Compromise:** Telerivet gets % of revenue (e.g., 15-25% platform + success fee) + board observer seat
- **But:** This needs founder conversation + legal docs

**Timeline:** Must resolve before Series A fundraising (week 8-12 is good window)
