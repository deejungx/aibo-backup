# Sahayata Profile & Context (Feb 16, 2026)

**Source:** Applicant profile document for funding/mentorship program

## Problem Statement
Businesses struggle to manage growing customer conversations across fragmented messaging channels:
- **Expectation gap:** Customers expect quick replies; delays lose leads
- **Fragmented channels:** Conversations spread across WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, Viber, SMS — context lost
- **Scalability limits:** Small teams can't handle volume without hiring
- **Manual repetitions:** Humans doing repetitive work = inefficiency
- **Lack of intelligence:** Existing automations are dumb; can't understand context or take action

## Solution: Sahayata AI
Omnichannel AI assistants for customer communication automation:
- **Omnichannel:** Works seamlessly across WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, Viber, SMS
- **Always-on:** 24/7 support without delays, any volume
- **Knowledge-based:** Answers grounded in org's own data
- **Action-oriented:** Capture leads, book appointments, trigger workflows
- **No-code:** Non-technical teams can deploy and manage
