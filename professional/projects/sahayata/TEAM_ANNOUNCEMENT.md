# Sahayata Team Announcement
## "Here's how we're working starting Monday"

---

## 👋 Hey team,

We've been grinding hard on the RAG pipeline and prompt modularization—great work. But we've realized we're missing something: **structure**. No planned sprints, no daily syncs, no retros. No way to connect what we build to what customers actually need.

Starting **Monday [DATE]**, we're running lean sprints with a ritual calendar designed for distributed teams like us.

**Good news:** This isn't bureaucracy. It's 90 minutes per week of structured time to:
- Know *why* we're building (customer discovery via Arun)
- Ship faster (clear goals each sprint)
- Appreciate each other (built-in kudos, not an afterthought)
- Bridge Symbiosis and Telerivet (shared rituals, one team)

---

## 📅 Your Weekly Calendar

**Monday 9 AM (30 min):** Sprint Planning
- What customers told us last week
- What we're shipping this week
- Who's doing what

**Tue-Fri (5 min each, async):** Daily Posts
- What you did yesterday, blocker (if any), what you're doing today
- Post in Slack, no reply needed unless you're stuck

**Thursday 9 AM (30 min):** Mid-week Huddle
- Quick demo of what shipped
- Any blockers? Let's unblock them
- How's the team vibe?

**Friday 9 AM (30 min):** Sprint Retro
- What went well? What was hard?
- **Kudos round:** Who helped you? (Specific, real appreciation)
- One small thing to try differently next sprint

**1st Friday of Month (60 min):** Monthly Recap
- What we shipped. Customer impact. Kudos ceremony. Next month's focus.

---

## 🎯 The Why

**We have:**
- A monthly recap idea ✅
- Great people grinding on hard problems ✅

**We DON'T have:**
- Customer signals → Sprint priorities (we build on gut feel)
- Structured retros (we never learn)
- Appreciation built in (it's random, not ritual)
- Cross-org rituals (Symbiosis ≠ Telerivet sometimes)

**This fixes it:**
- Arun tells us what customers need
- We build it, demo it, learn from it
- We appreciate each other explicitly
- We're one team with one sprint goal
